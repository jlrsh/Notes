type of [[Internal Forces|internal force]]; moment caused by shear force causing something to bend

$$\Delta M(x) = \int_{x_1}^{x_2} V(x) dx$$
*$M$ is bending moment, $V$ is [[Shear Force|Shear Force]]*
- internal couple that develops to keep the member in equilibrium under bending loads
	- balances applied loads by generating tension one side and compression on the other
