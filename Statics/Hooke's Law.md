Hooke's Law defines how much [[Stress|Stress]] is in a material proportional to [[Strain|Strain]]

### Axial Hooke's Law
$\sigma = E \cdot \epsilon$ where $\sigma$ is [[Axial Stress|axial stress]], $E$ is [[Young's Modulus|Young's Modulus]], and $\epsilon$ is [[Axial Strain|axial strain]]

### Shear Hooke's Law
$\tau = G * \gamma$ where $\tau$ is [[Shear Stress|shear stress]], $\gamma$ is [[Shear Strain|shear strain]], and $G$ is [[Shear Modulus|shear modulus]]
