Axial stress is [[Stress|Stress]] carried along a member's axis

$$\sigma = \frac{F}{A}$$
- when $\sigma < 0$, a body is in compression and when $\sigma > 0$ a body is in tension
	- similar to finding loads in [[Trusses|Trusses]]
- [[Hooke's Law|Hooke's Law]]: $\sigma = E \cdot \epsilon$ where $E$ is [[Young's Modulus]] and $\epsilon$ is [[Axial Strain|axial strain]]
