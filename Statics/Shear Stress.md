$$\tau = \frac{V}{A}$$
*$\tau$ = [[Shear Stress|Shear Stress]], $V$ = [[Shear Force|Shear Force]], $A$ = area*

[[Hooke's Law|Hooke's Law]]: $\tau = G * \gamma$ where $\tau$ is [[Shear Stress|shear stress]], $\gamma$ is [[Shear Strain|shear strain]], and $G$ is [[Shear Modulus|shear modulus]]
