[[Stress|Stress]] due to bending

$$\sigma_{bending}(y) = \frac{My}{I}$$ *$\sigma_{bending}$ is bending stress, $M$ is [[Bending Moment|Bending Moment]], $y$ is distance from neutral axis, $I$ is [[Second Moment of Area]]*
- Pure bending is when there is no net load but a bending moment, occurs when $V = 0 \cup M \neq 0$ where $V$ is [[Shear Force|Shear Force]] and M is [[Bending Moment|Bending Moment]]

### Radial Bending
$\sigma_{bending} = \epsilon_{bending}E = \frac{E}{\rho}y$
*$\rho$ = radius to neutral line distance, $y$ is distance from fiber to $\rho$*
