diagrams that display shear force or bending moment at a point for a body

### Shear Diagrams
- diagrams that display the amount of [[Shear Force|Shear Force]] at any given point
- shear is integral of load
- should start and end at 0, given beam is at equilibrium

### Bending Diagrams
- diagrams that display the [[Bending Moment|Bending Moment]] at any given point
- bending is integral of shear and double integral of load
- should start and end at 0, given beam is at equilibrium
