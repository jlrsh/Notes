[[Strain|Strain]] of fibers in a beam when bent (some are longer, some are shorter)

$$\epsilon_{bending} = \frac{\sigma_{bending}}{E} = \frac{-My}{EI}$$
*$\epsilon_{bending}$ = bending strain, $M$ = internal bending moment, $y$ = distance from neutral axis, $E$ = [[Young's Modulus|Young's Modulus]]*, $I$ = [[Second Moment of Area|Second Moment of Area]]

### Radial Bending
$\epsilon_{bending} = \frac{1}{\rho}y$
*$\rho$ = radius to neutral line distance, $y$ is distance from fiber to $\rho$*
