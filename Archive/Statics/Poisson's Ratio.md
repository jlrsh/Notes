Poisson's Ratio defines how much material deforms in other dimensions when load is applied

$$\epsilon_x = \epsilon_y = -\nu \epsilon_x$$
*$\epsilon$ = [[Shear Strain|Shear Strain]], $\nu$ = [[Poisson's Ratio|Poisson's]]*
- if the material is being pulled in the $x$ direction, Poisson's defines the deformation in $y$ and $z$
