impedance: measure of difficulty of AC flow through element

$$\widetilde{Z}(\omega) = \frac{\widetilde{V}(\omega)}{\widetilde I(\omega)}$$
*$\widetilde{Z}$ = impedance, $\widetilde{V}$ = voltage, $\widetilde{I}$ = current*

- impedance is function of frequency
- all elements obey [[Ohm's Law]] w/ impedance instead of [[Resistance|resistance]]

### Impedance of [[Resistors|Resistors]]
$$Z_R = R$$

### Impedance of [[Capacitors|Capacitors]]
$$Z_C = \frac{1}{j \omega C} = \frac{-j}{\omega C}$$
*$\omega$ = frequency, $j$ = $\sqrt{-1}$, $C$ = capacitance*

### Impedance of [[Inductors|Inductors]]
$$Z_L = j\omega L$$
*$\omega$ = frequency, $j$ = $\sqrt{-1}$, $L$ = inductance*
