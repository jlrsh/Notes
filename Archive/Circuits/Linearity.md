linearity: circuit response scales linearly with parts

- linear circuits have the following properties: 
	- homogeneity: $f(\alpha x) = \alpha f(x)$
	- additivity: $f(x + y) = f(x) + f(y)$


### Superposition
additivity means that for a circuit w/ multiple [[Sources|independent sources]], we can find total circuit response as sum of individual parts
